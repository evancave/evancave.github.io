About Me

I am a PhD student in philosophy at the University of British Columbia. My dissertation project defends a theory of normativity, according to which normativity is a disposition to affect how beings like us think and feel when we deliberate. Matt Bedke is my supervisor.

My other interests concern how we should subdivide different normative domains, and the ontological relationships between different normative properties, like reasons and fittingness.
